Renesas MCU Car Rally

The Renesas MCU Car Rally is an event held in Germany in which different Universities go head to head in order to make a small hand-built car navigate through a track by itself. The Group I worked with consists of 5 members, Daniel McCluskey, Thomas Hopkins, Zack Raeburn, Connor Liggins and Morgan James. During the course of the project, every team member contributed towards the completion of the car, including both the building of the car and the coding of the car. This project helped us since the learning experience opened up many opportunities for the future and expanded our knowledge of Software and Hardware integration.
